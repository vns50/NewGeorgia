Holidays in Georgia

Between the impregnable teeth of the crown of the Caucasus mountains and the sparkling eye of the Black Sea, lies a fairy-tale land. Name her Georgia.

Since time immemorial, Georgia has attracted travelers and poets, researchers and writers.

Once the legendary hero of ancient Greek mythology, Jasson, having overcome unheard-of trials, sailed to these shores in search of a priceless treasure - the Golden Fleece.

Sakartvelo tour

Today, the traveler does not have to overcome such difficult trials as the hero of Homer to get to desired shores and enjoy the treasure found. Modern airliners will comfortably deliver everyone wishing to one of the three international airports in Georgia.

The true treasure, the very Golden Fleece, for the modern traveler will be all this incredible cordial, hospitable and colorful country that captivates from the first acquaintance, at first sight anyone who comes here with an open heart in the sights of beauty.

Introducing a beautiful country

Where does the acquaintance begin? Of course, from the Georgian language, which is unusual and unaccustomed to the ear of a foreigner, as tart as young wine, a little guttural, charming with its color.

But if you are lucky enough to hear the many-voiced Georgian singing at least once, you will love this language truly, because you will hear how the soul of Georgia itself sounds.

Hospitality of Georgians

Gamarjoba! Georgia will greet you. Which means that you are welcome and that you are ready to share both bread and wine, and joy, and if necessary, sadness. Memorize a few words of this beautiful language. By this you show respect to the locals and win them over even more.

Respect for people, especially elders, is one of the hallmarks of real Georgians, absorbed with mother's milk, highly valued in Georgia since time immemorial.Georgia Nautilus

TOURS TO GEORGIA THE BEST CHOICE FOR REST

You may ask why vacation in Georgia? I will answer you. Because Georgia is an amazing country with more than five thousand years of history, rich original culture and fantastically beautiful nature. Everyone who rested in Georgia, will find its own zest.

For some, these are its forests, mountains, rivers, waterfalls and lakes, for some, ancient fortresses and monasteries, and for some narrow ancient streets of Georgian cities with cozy restaurants and cafes.

A variety of tours in Georgia from the company "Gorgia Nautilus" will satisfy any, even the most demanding taste. From two-three day study tours around the cities of Georgia, to two-week tours covering almost all regions and all interesting corners of Georgia.

Car and hiking, horseback and bicycle tours, land and water tours and routes will delight even the most sophisticated traveler.

Transfer

An important advantage will be the fact that your transfer in Georgia will be built as conveniently as possible, for you, in accordance with the route indicated in your tour, which will save you time and money.

Depending on those regions of Georgia that you have to rest, you can choose one of three arrival airports:

  • Tbilisi
  • Kutaisi
  • Batumi

drivers of our company will meet you in any of them and comfortably take you to your destination.

A variety of thematic locations and natural areas

A tour to Georgia will help you relax, enjoying the mountain landscapes of Kazbegi, with its grandiose ice peaks or inhaling the aromas of exotic plants in the national parks of Georgia Mtirala, Machakhela, Borjomi or botanical gardens of Batumi and Tbilisi.

You can relax on the shores of the gentle Black Sea, or you can explore the fantastic underground cave galleries Prometheus and Sataplia.

Feeling delight, you can fly fast on a zip-line suspension, or you can sit in a comfortable chair alone from thousands of excellent Georgian restaurants, relax and slowly savor fragrant Georgian wine.

Tours for lovers of culture and history of Georgia

For those who prefer to combine holidays in Georgia with the study of its original culture, against the backdrop of stunning natural landscapes, a tour to the mountainous Svaneti will be a real gift.

Ancient ancestral towers against the backdrop of mighty mountains will not leave you indifferent. You will see firsthand how Georgians lived hundreds of years ago.

For those wishing to delve into even greater antiquity during their holidays in Georgia, cave cities will reveal their secrets Vardzia and Uplistsikhe. On this tour of Georgia, you will get acquainted with the history of the emergence of these amazing cities carved into the rocks. The greatness of the Georgian kings who ruled the country at that time froze in their appearance.

Tours in Georgia will help you to feel the power of the ancient kings and the power of the Georgian state even more in general, excursions to ancient fortresses will help. And there are several dozen of them in Georgia. We will name only the most significant of them.

Tours in Georgia will help you to get to know the culture of the ancient state of Sakartvelo deeper cultural and historical tours of Tbilisi, with its unsurpassed architectural monuments, as well as a tour to the city-museum of Mtskheta - the ancient capital of Georgia, whose history goes back sixteen centuries and where the famous Svetitskhoveli Cathedral. And of course, what is a tour to Georgia without visiting museums:

  • Georgian National Museum

Authentic Gourmet Tour

Specialized gastronomic tours in Georgia are suitable for gourmets and connoisseurs of gastronomy. Most a popular region for this is Kakheti, with its famous Alazani Valley, wineries and wineries. cellars.

The national Georgian cuisine is truly magnificent and unforgettable. Under the guidance of experienced chefs, you you will be able to participate in the preparation of the famous Georgian dishes and then taste them washed down with fine wine.

georgia tour for romantics

Romantics will be delighted with a tour to Sighnaghi or the city of love, as it is also called in Georgia, Listen the world-famous love story of the famous Georgian artist Nikolo Pirosmani, relax walking along quiet, well-groomed and incredibly atmospheric streets of Sighnaghi, sit on the summer terrace of the restaurant overlooking Alazani Valley, believe me, it's worth it to come here.

Another of the most romantic and at the same time iconic places, which is definitely worth a visit during rest in Georgia - Jvari Monastery, located near the city of Mtskheta, on a steep cliff above the confluence of the rivers Aragvas and Kura, sung by Mikhail Lermontov in the poem "Mtsyri". Stunning view from its height, attracts travelers for a long time.
